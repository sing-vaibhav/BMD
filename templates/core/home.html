Our Story

Art Meets Opportunity

The name "Rang Aura" is derived from Rang (color) and Aura (unique energy). Together, we symbolize the emotional and artistic energy that colors bring to life.


We bridge the gap between talented painters who struggle to find buyers and art collectors who seek authentic, original artwork — creating a trusted digital ecosystem where art meets opportunity.
